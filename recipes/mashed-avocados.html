Mashed Avocados Recipe

Description

This creamy mashed avocado recipe will delight all of your guests!

Ingredients

  • 2 medium size avocados
  • Powdered garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Peel the avocados
  2. Add salt, pepper, and powdered garlic to taste
  3. Mash the avocados until no longer whole
  4. Add olive oil to taste
  5. Continue mashing the avocados until the oil is integrated
  6. Enjoy!
