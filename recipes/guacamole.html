Guacamole

Guacamole with chips

This recipe will teach you how to make creamy, delicious guacamole for you and your friends or family!

It's really easy to make, and takes only around 10 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 Ripe avocado per person
  • 1 tsp of olive oil
  • 1 tsp of lemon juice
  • 1 small garlic clove per avocado
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut the avocados and scoop them out
  2. Smash them with a fork or a different smashing tool
  3. Add diced garlic, lemon and oil
  4. Salt to taste
  5. Done! Serve and enjoy!
